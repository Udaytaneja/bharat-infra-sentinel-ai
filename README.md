# 📌 Problem Statement

India's infrastructure ecosystem faces significant challenges:

* Road potholes
* Water leakages
* Damaged public assets
* Broken streetlights
* Waste accumulation
* Delayed maintenance response
* Lack of predictive maintenance systems
* Poor contractor accountability
* Fragmented reporting systems

Most governance systems remain **reactive**, meaning authorities act only after complaints are received and manually processed.

This leads to:

❌ Increased maintenance costs
❌ Infrastructure deterioration
❌ Resource wastage
❌ Citizen dissatisfaction
❌ Slow decision-making

# 💡 Our Solution

**Bharat Infra Sentinel AI** transforms traditional complaint management into an **AI-driven predictive infrastructure intelligence platform**.

The platform combines:

* Citizen Reporting
* Artificial Intelligence
* Risk Assessment
* Predictive Analytics
* GIS Intelligence
* Contractor Monitoring
* Governance Dashboards

to enable **Predictive Governance** instead of Reactive Governance.

Create a `.env` file:

```env
GEMINI_API_KEY=YOUR_GEMINI_API_KEY

VITE_SUPABASE_URL=YOUR_SUPABASE_URL

VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=YOUR_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
```

 AI Decision-Making Workflow

When a complaint is submitted, the AI engine performs:

Step 1 — Incident Understanding

Input:

Citizen Description
Uploaded Image
Geographic Coordinates

Example:

Large pothole near market road causing traffic congestion.
Step 2 — Infrastructure Classification
| Category                  | Example             |
| ------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Road Damage               | Potholes            |
| Water Infrastructure      | Leakages            |
| Electrical Infrastructure | Streetlight Failure |
| Waste Management          | Garbage Overflow    |
| Structural Issues         | Cracks              |
Step 3 — Severity Assessment

Severity Levels:

Low
Medium
High
Critical
Factors:

Public Safety Risk
Asset Damage Potential
Traffic Impact
Population Exposure
Step 4 — Risk Score Generation

Formula:

Risk Score =
Severity +
Citizen Impact +
Failure Probability +
Infrastructure Criticality

Output:

Risk Score: 87/100
Step 5 — Department Recommendation

AI automatically routes complaints.

Example:

Pothole
↓
Public Works Department
Streetlight Failure
↓
Electrical Department
Water Leakage
↓
Water Supply Department🚀 Future Enhancements
